Chairman Message

The chief purpose of education, according to our founder
B. Subrahmanyam Reddy, is to cultivate individuals into enlightened and socially responsible citizens with a holistic understanding of life. He firmly believes that education should nurture individuals physically, mentally, socially, and spiritually, fostering the development of an integral personality—a model of humanity. At Little Angels Public School, we are guided by this philosophy. Our founder emphasizes that human excellence is built upon a foundation of high academic standards and entrenched human values. Therefore, academic excellence serves as our guiding principle, complemented by a strong emphasis on moral and ethical values. Perfection and excellence are the cornerstones of our educational approach. Through perseverance and character development, we strive to instill in our students the qualities needed to excel not only academically but also as compassionate and responsible members of society. Under the able guidance of our founder, Little Angels Public School is committed to upholding these principles, ensuring that every student receives a well-rounded education that prepares them to navigate the complexities of the world with confidence, integrity, and excellence.

Little Angels Public School was founded in the year 1985 with an initial enrollment of 150 students in Gudur town under the auspices of the Paragathi Education Society
