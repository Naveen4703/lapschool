CBSE Curriculum at Little Angels Public School Message

At Little Angels Public School, we follow the curriculum prescribed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which is renowned for its comprehensive and well-structured approach to education. Our adherence to the CBSE curriculum ensures that our students receive a high-quality education that is recognized nationally and internationally. Here's an overview of our CBSE curriculum:

● Well-Defined Syllabus

- The CBSE curriculum provides a well-defined syllabus for each subject, ensuring consistency and clarity in teaching and learning.

- The syllabus is designed to cater to the diverse learning needs of students while maintaining academic rigor.

● Focus on Holistic Development

- The CBSE curriculum emphasizes the holistic development of students, encompassing academic, social, emotional, and physical aspects.

- It encourages the integration of co-curricular and extracurricular activities to foster creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

● Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE)

- CBSE follows the Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) system, which assesses students' progress throughout the academic year.

- CCE includes both formative and summative assessments, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of students' academic performance and overall development.

● Flexibility and Adaptability

- The CBSE curriculum offers flexibility in the choice of subjects and courses, allowing students to pursue their interests and talents.

- It adapts to changing educational trends and requirements, incorporating innovations and best practices in teaching and learning.

● Emphasis on Values and Life Skills:

- CBSE promotes the inculcation of values and life skills among students, preparing them to face real-world challenges with integrity and resilience.

- It integrates value education and life skills training into the curriculum, fostering ethical awareness, empathy, and social responsibility.

● Alignment with National Standards

- The CBSE curriculum is aligned with national educational standards and objectives, ensuring uniformity and consistency in educational practices across the country.

- It prepares students for various national-level competitive exams and entrance tests, equipping them with the knowledge and skills required for higher education and career pursuits. At Little Angels Public School, we are committed to implementing the CBSE curriculum effectively to provide our students with a well- rounded education that prepares them for success in the global arena. Through innovative teaching methodologies, experiential learning opportunities, and personalized support, we strive to nurture confident, competent, and compassionate individuals who are ready to make a positive impact on society.

Little Angels Public School was founded in the year 1985 with an initial enrollment of 150 students in Gudur town under the auspices of the Paragathi Education Society
