Welcome To Little Angels Public School

Little Angels Public School was founded in the year 1985 with an initial enrollment of 150 students in Gudur town under the auspices of the Paragathi Education Society. Initially offering classes from LKG to Class 6, the school expanded its facilities and offerings over the years. Two years later, the school relocated to Chellakur, where it acquired seven acres of land to accommodate its growing student population, which had increased to 300. Since then, the school has undergone significant development and expansion. Presently, it has a student strength of 1500 students from LKG to 10th grade, with a faculty comprising 75 teachers. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

Chairman Message

The chief purpose of education, according to our founder
B. Subrahmanyam Reddy, is to cultivate individuals into enlightened and socially responsible citizens with a holistic understanding of life. He firmly believes that education should nurture individuals physically, mentally, socially, and spiritually, fostering the development of an integral personality—a model of humanity.

At Little Angels Public School, we are guided by this philosophy. Our founder emphasizes that human excellence is built upon a foundation of high academic standards and entrenched human values. Therefore, academic excellence serves as our guiding principle, complemented by a strong emphasis on moral and ethical values. Perfection and excellence are the cornerstones of our educational approach. Through perseverance and character development, we strive to instill in our students the qualities needed to excel not only academically but also as compassionate and responsible members of society. Under the able guidance of our founder, Little Angels Public School is committed to upholding these principles, ensuring that every student receives a well-rounded education that prepares them to navigate the complexities of the world with confidence, integrity, and excellence.

Correspondent Message

As the Correspondent of Little Angels Public School, I am honored to address our esteemed students, parents, teachers, and staff members. Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it is about nurturing individuals into responsible and enlightened citizens who contribute positively to society. At Little Angels Public School, we firmly believe in this philosophy. Our mission is to provide a conducive learning environment where students can flourish academically, socially, and emotionally. We strive to instill in them the values of integrity, compassion, and resilience, which are essential for success in life. As Correspondent, I am committed to ensuring that Little Angels Public School continues to uphold the highest standards of education and ethical conduct. I am dedicated to fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and inclusivity within our school community. Together, let us work towards the holistic development of our students and empower them to become confident, compassionate, and competent individuals who will make a positive difference in the world. Thank you for entrusting us with the education and well-being of your children. With your continued support, we will continue to strive for excellence in all that we do.
