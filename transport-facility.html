Transport Facilities at Little Angels Public School

Little Angels Public School is dedicated to ensuring the safety and convenience of our students by providing reliable transportation services. We understand the importance of accessibility for students coming from various parts of Gudur town and its surrounding areas. Here's how we facilitate transportation for our students:

1. Fleet of Buses:

- We maintain a fleet of seven buses equipped with modern amenities to ensure the comfort and safety of our students during their commute to and from school.
- Our buses are well-maintained, regularly serviced, and operated by trained drivers and support staff who prioritize the well-being of our students.

2. Extensive Coverage:

- Our bus routes cover a wide area in and around Gudur town, ensuring accessibility for students residing in different neighborhoods and localities.
- We strive to accommodate the transportation needs of as many students as possible, minimizing travel time and inconvenience for families.

3. Safety Measures:

- The safety of our students is our top priority. Our buses are equipped with safety features such as seat belts, emergency exits, first aid kits, and GPS tracking systems.
- We adhere to strict safety protocols and guidelines to ensure a secure and supervised transportation experience for all students.

4. Trained Staff:

- Our bus drivers and attendants undergo thorough background checks and training in defensive driving techniques, passenger safety, and emergency procedures.
- They are responsible for maintaining discipline and order on the buses, ensuring that students follow safety rules and regulations at all times.

5. Communication Channels:

- We maintain open communication channels with parents and guardians to provide updates on bus routes, schedules, delays, and other relevant information.
- Parents can contact our transportation department or school administration for any inquiries or concerns regarding transportation services.

6. Convenience and Accessibility:

- Our transportation services provide a convenient and stressfree option for families, allowing students to focus on their studies without worrying about transportation logistics.
- We strive to make our transportation services accessible and inclusive, accommodating students with special needs or specific requirements whenever possible.

7. Continuous Improvement:

- We regularly evaluate and review our transportation services to identify areas for improvement and ensure the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and reliability.
- Feedback from parents, students, and staff is welcomed and taken into consideration as we strive to enhance the overall transportation experience for our school community.

At Little Angels Public School, we are committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient transportation services to our students, enabling them to access quality education while minimizing the challenges associated with commuting. Our transportation facilities reflect our dedication to the well-being and convenience of our school community.

Little Angels Public School was founded in the year 1985 with an initial enrollment of 150 students in Gudur town under the auspices of the Paragathi Education Society
